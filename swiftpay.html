SwiftPay – Fintech Mobile App

A secure and frictionless digital payment experience.

Overview

SwiftPay is a fintech mobile app concept designed to simplify peer-to-peer transfers, bill payments, and wallet management while maintaining strong security standards.

The Problem

Many financial apps feel complex, overloaded with features, and intimidating for first-time users. The challenge was designing a clean, easy-to-navigate experience without sacrificing functionality.

Target Users

Young professionals, students, and tech-savvy users who need fast, reliable digital transactions.

My Role

UX research, user flow mapping, wireframing, visual design, and design system creation.
