Community Platform for Tech Enthusiasts

A structured web platform designed to centralize tech discussions and engagement.

Overview

This personal project explores how to create a centralized platform for developers and tech enthusiasts to connect, share ideas, and engage in structured discussions.

The Problem

Many tech communities rely on scattered tools such as messaging apps and social media groups. These platforms often lack structure, making it difficult to organize conversations and track important resources.

Target Users

Developers, designers, tech learners, and startup enthusiasts looking for a focused and organized digital community.

My Role

UX structure planning, navigation design, layout system creation, and interface design.
